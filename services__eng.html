Brow Service

MICROBLADING

Microblading requires the technic use a small tool with teeny, tiny needles, instead of using a traditional semi-permanent machine.
These needles are used to manually draw delicate hair-like strokes while also depositing pigment under your skin.
The effect will be realistic-looking brow hairs that don’t wash off.

POWERED OMBRE

Powder Ombre brow are a permanent powdered brow technique for clients who prefer fuller brows compared to hair-like strokes.
This technique is similar to a daily makeup look. The tails of the brow are darker and fade into a light frontal area to the brow giving a perfect Ombre makeup effect.
The effects will be waterproof, smudge-proof and completely natural.
